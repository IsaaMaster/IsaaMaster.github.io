🚀Research

I am a undergraduate chemical and physical researcher studying the interactions of thin films of molecules absorbed onto an Al2O3 crystal at low temperatures.

Currently, I am working with the Nishimura Research Group at Westmont College to gain a better understanding of the disorder to order transtion of biphenyl and its interactions with organic molecules.

One major way I contributed was by using my CS skills to write Python scripts to automate the modeling of Avrami crystal growth. Ultimately, this optimized processing times by over 100-fold and ensured the integrity and accuracy of the results.
